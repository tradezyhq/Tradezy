Section 3
How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing the service: Creating accounts, displaying listings, processing trades, facilitating communication between traders
  • Safety and security: Detecting fraud, scam behavior, platform bypass attempts, and prohibited conduct
  • Dispute resolution: Reviewing messages, trade history, and evidence to resolve disputes fairly
  • Payment processing: Charging platform fees, holding escrow, processing withdrawals via Stripe
  • Communications: Sending trade notifications, offer alerts, dispute updates, and important account information
  • Identity verification: Verifying phone numbers via SMS and coordinating ID verification via Stripe Identity
  • Legal compliance: Meeting tax reporting requirements, responding to lawful government requests
  • Platform improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve features and fix issues

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it in ways unrelated to operating the Tradezy platform.

Section 4
Data We Share

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other traders: Your public profile (display name, general location, ratings, verified badges, and listings) is visible to other users. Your exact address, phone number, email, and full legal name are never shared with other traders.
  • With Stripe: Payment information, identity verification data, and transaction amounts are shared with Stripe to process fees and hold escrow. See Section 5.
  • With Stripe Identity: If you choose to verify your government ID, your document is shared with Stripe Identity for processing only. See Section 6.
  • With our AI system (Anthropic / Claude): Dispute-related messages, trade data, and evidence may be analyzed by our Claude AI dispute assistant. This is covered in our Terms of Service Section 13.
  • With law enforcement: We may share data when required by a valid legal order, subpoena, or when we have a good-faith belief that disclosure is necessary to prevent fraud, protect safety, or comply with legal obligations.
  • In a business transfer: If Tradezy is acquired, merged, or sold, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity subject to the same privacy protections.
✅
We never sell your personal data. We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies. Tradezy products are ad-free and we do not monetize your personal information.
Section 5
Stripe & Payment Processing

All payment processing on Tradezy is handled by Stripe, Inc. Tradezy does not store your full credit card numbers, debit card numbers, bank account numbers, or other sensitive financial data. This information is transmitted directly to Stripe using SSL/TLS encryption and is subject to PCI DSS Level 1 compliance standards.

When you add a payment method, Stripe stores it securely and provides Tradezy with only a token and the last 4 digits for display purposes. Tradezy uses this token to charge platform fees — we never see or store your full payment details.

Your use of Stripe is also subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy, available at stripe.com/privacy. We encourage you to review it.

Tradezy receives from Stripe: transaction records, fee amounts, payout confirmations, and dispute-related financial data. This information is retained for tax reporting and legal compliance purposes for a minimum of 7 years.

Section 6
Identity Verification & Government ID

Tradezy's identity verification program is powered by Stripe Identity. If you choose to complete government ID verification, here is exactly what happens with your data:

  1. You upload your ID and/or selfie through the Tradezy platform
  2. The images are transmitted securely to Stripe Identity over an encrypted connection
  3. Stripe Identity processes the document using automated and potentially human review
  4. Stripe returns a verification result (pass/fail) to Tradezy
  5. Tradezy only stores the result — verified or not verified
  6. Your ID images and selfie are permanently deleted by Stripe after the verification is complete per Stripe Identity's data handling policy
🪪
Tradezy never sees, stores, or has access to your actual ID images or selfie photos. These are processed entirely by Stripe Identity. If you have questions about how Stripe handles your ID, review Stripe Identity's privacy documentation at stripe.com/privacy.
Section 7
Location Data

Tradezy is a hyperlocal platform and requires a general sense of your location to show you relevant nearby trades. Here is how we handle location:

  • What we collect: Your town, city, or ZIP code — never your street address or precise GPS coordinates unless you explicitly grant permission for GPS auto-detection
  • What other traders see: Only your general area — for example "Austin, TX" or "Within 10 miles" — never your exact address or coordinates
  • GPS auto-detection: If you tap "Use my current location" we request your device's GPS coordinates temporarily to identify your city/area — this precise location is not stored on our servers
  • Why we need it: To show you listings within your chosen radius and verify that traders are genuinely local

You can update or change your location at any time through your account settings.

Section 8
SMS & Communications

By providing your phone number and completing SMS verification, you consent to receive the following types of messages from Tradezy:

  • Verification codes: One-time codes for account verification and login
  • Trade alerts: Notifications when you receive an offer, a trade is accepted, or a trade is confirmed complete
  • Dispute updates: Notifications about dispute filings, status changes, and resolutions
  • Security alerts: Unusual account activity, login from new device
  • Account notices: Important policy updates, account warnings

Standard SMS rates may apply. You can opt out of non-essential SMS communications in your account settings. You cannot opt out of security and verification messages while your account is active.

Tradezy will never share your phone number with other traders or third parties for marketing purposes.

Section 9
Message Monitoring & Bypass Detection

All messages sent through the Tradezy in-platform messaging system are subject to automated monitoring. This monitoring serves the following safety purposes:

  • Bypass detection: Scanning for phone numbers, email addresses, social media handles, and payment app usernames shared with the intent to trade off-platform
  • Fraud detection: Identifying patterns consistent with scam behavior or fraudulent listings
  • Prohibited item detection: Monitoring listings and messages for references to prohibited items including firearms, alcohol, tobacco, drugs, and other banned goods
  • Dispute evidence: Preserving message logs as evidence in dispute resolution cases
  • Safety: Detecting harassment, threats, or other prohibited conduct
💬
By using Tradezy's messaging system, you consent to automated monitoring of your messages for safety and compliance purposes. Messages flagged by our system may be reviewed by our AI dispute assistant and, in serious cases, by a human admin. Do not share personal contact information in Tradezy messages.

Message logs are retained for 2 years to support potential dispute resolution and legal compliance needs.

Section 10
Cookies & Tracking

Tradezy uses cookies and similar technologies to operate the platform. Here is what we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for login sessions, security tokens, and basic platform functionality. Cannot be disabled.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your location radius, display preferences, and saved settings.
  • Analytics cookies: Understand how users navigate the platform so we can improve it. We use privacy-respecting analytics that do not share data with advertising networks.

Tradezy does not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or any cookies that share your data with advertisers or data brokers. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Section 11
Data Security

Tradezy takes the security of your personal information seriously. We implement the following measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers
  • Hashed passwords — your password is never stored in readable form
  • Stripe PCI DSS Level 1 compliance for all payment data
  • Limited access controls — only authorized personnel can access user data
  • Regular security reviews of platform infrastructure
  • Automatic session expiration after periods of inactivity

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, Tradezy will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by applicable law.

Section 12
Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Active account data: Retained for as long as your account is active
  • Trade and payment records: 7 years minimum for tax and legal compliance
  • Message logs: 2 years
  • Dispute records: 3 years after resolution
  • Deleted account data: Most data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request. Financial records retained for 7 years per legal requirements.
  • IP and security logs: 90 days
  • ID verification results: Retained as long as your account is active. Deleted when account is closed.

Section 14
California Residents — CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell (we do not sell data)
  • Right to delete personal information we have collected from you
  • Right to opt out of sale — Tradezy does not sell personal information, so this right is already satisfied
  • Right to non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

To submit a CCPA request, email privacy@tradezyhq.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.
