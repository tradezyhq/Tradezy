Introducing Ship-It —
Trade Across State Lines
Not every great trade happens in your backyard. Ship-It lets fully verified traders send items anywhere in the country — safely, tracked, and protected by Tradezy the whole way.
1
Make a Ship-It offer on any listing — local or nationwide
2
Seller accepts — 10% platform fee collected at acceptance
3
Buyer sends payment via Zelle, PayPal, Venmo, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Cash App
4
Seller ships with tracking — both parties confirm delivery to close the trade
🗓️ Ship-It launches July 31 — ID verification required for both parties
🪪
ID Verified Only
Both parties must be ID verified before a Ship-It trade can happen. No exceptions.
📦
Full Tracking
Tracking number recorded in-app the moment the seller ships. Both traders can follow the package.
💸
Peer-to-Peer Payment
Money moves directly between traders. Tradezy never holds your funds — just your 10% platform fee.

Dispute Protection
Confirmation screenshots, tracking records, and Tradezy support — protection built into every step.
